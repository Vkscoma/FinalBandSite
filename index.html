ABOUT US

Hotel Garden is a Sacramento based Indie Rock band that met through a chance encounter on Craigslist. Their musical style draws inspiration from artists such as Peach Pit, Turnover, and The Backseat Lovers. With each show, the band strives to connect with audiences through their music and create a memorable experience.
